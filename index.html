About Me

I manage a team of Implementation Engineers at a Financial Services software company called Bottomline Technologies. My team is responsible for implementing all of our Hosted Banking web application products in our company datacenter. Team functions include making database and property file configuration changes, establishing connectivty to third party services, and implementing integration configuration to map data to and from external parties.
